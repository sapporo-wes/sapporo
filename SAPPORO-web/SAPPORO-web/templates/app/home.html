SAPPORO

SAPPORO is a job execution system for always reproducing Batch Jobs. In SAPPORO, the concept of CI/CD is introduced to management of batch jobs. In other words, SAPPORO is intended to test wheter "batch jobs are always executable" and "output by batch job is always reproduced". SAPPORO can be deployed to various environments (on-premises, cloud, cluster. etc.) and can work with various job schedulers. SAPPORO can be easily deployed and maneged.
